Artist bio

A Designer

Welcome! This is Cecilia Zheng. She currently studies Digital Media at RMIT Melbourne City Campus.
Since she was a child, her mind never settled on what she would like to pursue in her future life. Despite having an interest in drawing and photography, she never considered emerging her hobbies and career together until she was 18.
Cecilia has a broad range of interest in digital media. She hopes to further investigate areas such as animation, video-making, photography, 2D-design and more!
